GlimpseWearables

You pick the moment, we'll capture it

Keep Your Eyes Where They Are Supposed To Be!

Glimpse offers a new way to capture all of your favorite moments at a concert, without ever having to take your eyes off the stage.

Rather than pulling out your phone, which is not only distracting to yourself, but everyone around you and the artists performing. We have created a stylish headband with a high quality camera embedded in it.

The Tech

Our Devices are Wicked

Capable of patented "RetroActive Recording", our devices are able to record 15 seconds before and after you click the record button.

Along with the retroactive recording, each device is capable of live streaming 32 FPS 720p video to our platform.

Not only are the devices serving as a recording device, but they are also a stylish bandana that adds a layer of style to the users outfit.

Our Events

As a company, we have done user and device testing at 6 concerts and festivals where we were able to collect live data from our devices.

With each event that we have gone to, we have learned a lot about the devices we have created, the experiences that our users have had, and the endless possibilities that our technologies bring to the music industry.

We have the goal starting February 1st to attend at least one 1 concert a week to continue testing devices and get feedback from our users.

Members

The members that are involved in Glimpsewearables can be catergorized as two different types of people. User and Artists

The User is an every day concert goer or groupie that attends a concert while wearing our headband, capturing their experience on our devices and having their media available on the platform we have created for them.

Meanwhile the Artists are getting ALL of the content captured from the attendees perspective that they would not have access to without our services/devices. Artists, or whoever is in charge of the artist content, have access to the data we are visualizing and categorizing that has never before been captured and quanitified.

User Experience

In the Alpha phase that we are in right now, we are focusing on our concert goers that are wearing the headbands to the concerts of their choice.

Our Users/Device Testers are able to capture content at a concert that they would normally have to pull their phone out to record, taking their eyes off that precious moment they will never get back. After the concert, instead of sifting through hours of mediocre content on their phone, each user recieves the BEST moments they recorded, and a montage with the best moments from the concert personally curated by our team.

As we continue to grow as a company, our user base will expand from the device testers, to anyone that is interested in viewing the content that we are capturing at each event.

The current site you are on right now is by NO MEANS the site that our users are going to use to view their concert content from themselves and their friends. We are developing a much nicer site for our users to view their content on. This is simply a intermediary site that we are using while the users site is being created.

Artist Experience

Our Artists get the full Rock Star treatment

For the Tour Package, our artist gets a dedicated team equipped with 30+ devices for collecting never before seen footage from each concert on the road. Compiling all of that content into an EPIC montage of what attending their concert looks like, from the perspective of the fans, managers, backstage groupies, bartenders and artists themselve! They will also recieve a custom made website that highlights every stop on the road, and all of the cool things that happen in their lives.

To go along with the new footage, they will also recieve data about all of their sets they have never been able to collect before.

Admin

You are currently in the Admin Portal for the backend services of our platform. This is where we have access to all of the information that is being stored in the database.

We are currently polishing up the backend services for the API, while simultaniously creating a high quality web application dedicated to the everyday User/Artist/Guest of the site.

Metrics

The amount of data that we are collecting for each concert has never been seen before!

We are able to not only track the points in a concert that the fans loved, but are able to get 30+ different views of that exact moment when the entire crowd went wild.
